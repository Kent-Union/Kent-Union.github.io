    Adding a news article

    Warning

    This is a very, very crowded system on the website if you are an admin unfortunately and removing things from here could have unknowing consequences. This guide is for adding articles assuming you know where you want it to appear.

    1. Navigate to the page with the news feed where you wish your article to appear. This example follows adding news to the homepage, which has an Organisation name of Greenwich and Kent Students' Unions Together.
    2. Click Control Panel in the top right corner and then Edit [Organisation name] News
    3. Click Add new article
    1. Fill in Title with the name of your article that will appear. Keep this short and to the point (30 characters is ideal).
    2. Remove your name from the Byline
    3. Keep Allow Non Members to read ticked unless you require them to log in first

    4. Set the Display article dates from the date to and from you wish it to be visible

      Info

      Unless the information is massively time sensitive and would be irrelevant in the near future, it is best to have an expiry a long way off so that it stays accessible for users.

    5. Turn on or off Allow comments, Enable sharing and Enable ratings if you wish to

    6. Leave Tags blank, you are already setting the location of the article in steps 1 and 2

      Info

      Images for news articles on the homepage are 600 x 400 and are cropped to fit, but stick with the correct size or you might lose part of the image

    7. Upload the image you have prepared by clicking Select image > Choose File

    8. Find the image you want in the popup and click Open > Upload
    9. Select the image you just uploaded from the browser and click Insert
    10. You shouldn't need to worry about Thumbnail resizing if you are using the correct size already, otherwise change it to Stretch

    11. Fill in the Body. This is the most important part, the content to your article.

      Info

      The Body editor maintains formatting from Microsoft Word if you use the Paste from Word button but you might find it easier to paste the content in, highlight it all and click Remove Format (which looks like an underlined Tx) then format your content in the box itself.

    12. If you are adept at HTML code, you can click Source and paste it directly in

    13. If you are inserting images, click the landscape button Image > Browse Server > Choose File > Select the file you want to add > Open > Upload
    14. Select the image in the asset browser and it closes the window, showing you options to edit the height and width etc before you Insert.
    15. Tick This article is approved and then Save
    16. Check the article is appearing where you wanted it to at the beginning

    If you want to set the URL

    Info

    All articles made on the website have a URL in the format https://www.gkunions.co.uk/news/article/[ID number]/[URL you are about to set].

    1. Follow steps 1 and 2 from before to get to the News Admin page for the appropriate news feed
    2. Click the article you wish to set a URL for
    3. Fill in the URL field, the third one down, with a friendly URL, which should not contain spaces or be too long
    4. Click Save at the bottom and check the article now appears at the new URL
