    Creating a News Article

    Creating a News Article

    Go to Website > News and click + News in the top right

    Under News

    1. Title: The display name for the article (ie, Record turnout for Elections!)

    2. URL Name: The web address that appears in the web-address bar

      Note

      If you’re going to want to use it in print keep it short and tidy (i.e. recordturnout)

    3. Thumbnail: This image must be 360px x 260px and will appear wherever the is a relevant news feed

    4. Image: This image must be 1920px x 700px and will appear in the article page itself

      Note

      See Resizing an Image for how to create the correctly sized image

    5. Tagline: A short sentence to describe the article (i.e. 99% students vote!) which must be 75 characters or less

    6. Snippet: One sentence extract from the article, usually the first line of text

    7. Body: This is the actual content of your article

      It accepts HTML input if you click Source and can format articles more fully with some practice

      Otherwise, just treat it like a normal word processor – if you’re copying from Microsoft Word, it will keep most of your formatting but double check it for consistency in font, size, colour etc.

    Under Publishing

    1. Active: Ensure this box is checked

    2. Start Date: Enter the start date from in the format shown which is dd-mm-yyyy hh:mm

      If you would like the article to appear straight away select a date and time prior to your current time

    3. End Date: If you would like the article to disappear after a specific date enter it here

      If you’re happy for it to remain up leave the field empty

    Select a ‘parent category’ to make it appear all of the child pages in that group for wider reach of your news article.

    Double check the article has:

    1. Appeared in the correct place(s) and ‘Modify’ the article Categories if needed

    2. Check the URL is correct by visiting the actual article page at https://kentunion.co.uk/news/article/{URL-Name}

    3. To modify your article or remove it from view go to Website > News and click Modify on your article

      Refrain from clicking Remove on this screen as your article will be unrecoverable. Instead click Modify and uncheck Active.

    Common issues and solutions

    Sometimes when you Submit, errors will appear in red by their relevant sections on the page. When you correct any of these issues you must also reupload the Thumbnail and Image.
